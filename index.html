About the game

Find the weapon

On some of the missions you will be given a choice of weapons to choose from! Don't forget that the monster is not easy to defeat!

Solve the mystery

Explore labyrinths on various missions. In them you can find anything, even your death...

Beware the monster

The monster will be waiting for you on missions. Play its game or it will feast on you!

Walk, see, explore

You are an agent! Your task is to collect anomalies, study the labyrinth and listen very carefully...

Game description

You are a field agent for an organization IAEA. Your task will be to study the labyrinth and complete tasks in the labyrinth. This is collecting data on anomalies, collecting supplies and installing instruments to study the soil in dangerous areas. However, you are not alone in this maze... Beware...
